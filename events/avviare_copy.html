Avviare

Registration

Prizes worth : ₹ 10000/-

Entry Fee: : ₹ 500/- Per Team

Event Description:

In this dynamic world of Business, each day brings to us a new challenge. Your decisions and breakthrough can actually change the future of the company you are associated with. The situations of distress in which the industry experts are required to resolve the problems, and to maintain their rationality can be a great challenge in itself.

The best way you can make yourself ready for this phase is by participating in a challenging Case Study Competition that is actually faced by a start-up. Avviare is about evaluating and solving the problems that are critical to answer for an entrepreneur or business person at a certain stage. These competitions guarantee to provide a real world entrepreneurship/management experience and test your skills over a really challenging case to solve.

So ITELF SCIT presents to you the platform and a challenging case study competition.

The Case to be solved would be mailed to all the registered participants.

Round 1:

This will be an elimination round, where the case study will be shared only to the registered participants. The contenders are expected to review this case study and make a power point presentation keeping in mind the business model, market analysis, resolution feasibility and time and cost of implemention. Last date for submission of this presentation is 14th September 11:59 PM.

Round 2:

6 teams will be selected on 14th September and will be invited to give their presentations on 17th September in front of the panellists which will also include the business owner facing the problem.In this round each team will be allotted 15 mins to pitch their respective resolution in front of the jury.

Round 3:

The last round will be a question and answer round with all the six individual teams, where the jury will test and evaluate the feasibility of the suggested proposition.

Other Details:

  • Prize money of Rs 10,000 will be awarded to the team with the best resolution.
  • Entry Fee of Rs 500 will be charged for the registration of each team.

Rules:

  • The slide limit is 10 slides including Introduction(1 slide) and Conclusion(1 slide) and excluding appendices.
  • Each team should consist of a minimum of 1 and maximum of 3 members.
  • Naming convention for the PPT : College name_Team name_ITELF2016.
  • Feel free to make assumptions, provided they are logical and coherent extrapolations of the situations described in the case document.
  • Plagiarism will attract penalties.
  • No limit on the number of participants from each college.
  • The event is open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students.
  • The decision of the organizing team and the jury will be final and abiding.
  • One member cannot be a part of two teams, all team members have to be from same college.
  • Presentation can be done only one person at a time.
  • All the presentations submitted will be treated as a property of ITELF 2016/SCIT.
  • The deadline for the submission is 14th September 2016, 11:59 PM.
